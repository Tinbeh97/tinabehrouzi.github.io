Tina Behrouzi

Welcome to my personal profile! I am a PhD student in Computer Science, working under the supervision of Anna Goldenberg at the University of Toronto. My research focuses on the intersection of AI and Healthcare. My research interests include Time-series analysis, self-supervised learning, and graph machine learning.
